<center> Instead of  "<s TAB" </center>
	<s and company too fast ,use abbrev isrc and ijava in org-mode for Literate programming.
<!---more--->

```
(setq-default abbrev-mode t)

(define-skeleton 1src
    "Input src"
    ""
    "#+BEGIN_SRC emacs-lisp \n"
    _ "\n"
    "#+END_SRC")
(define-skeleton 1java
    "Input src"
    ""
    "#+HEADER: :classname\n"
    "#+BEGIN_SRC java \n"
    _ "\n"
    "#+END_SRC")
(define-abbrev org-mode-abbrev-table "isrc" "" '1src)
(define-abbrev org-mode-abbrev-table "ijava" "" '1java)
```
